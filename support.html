Frequently asked questions

How do I convert a unit? ▼
Tap a category on the home screen, then tap the unit you want to convert from. Use the built-in keypad to enter a value and all other units will update instantly.
How do I reorder units? ▼
Tap Edit in the top-right corner of the converter screen. You can then drag units to your preferred order. Tap Done when finished.
How do I mark a unit as favourite? ▼
Tap the star icon on the right side of any unit row. Favourited units are pinned to the top of the list for quick access.
I purchased "Remove Ads" but ads are still showing. ▼
Go to Settings → Premium → Restore purchases inside the app. This will verify your purchase with Apple and remove all ads. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device? ▼
Open the app, go to Settings → Premium → Restore purchases. Sign in with the same Apple ID and your purchase will be restored at no extra cost.
Is Measuro available on iPad or Mac? ▼
Measuro is currently available for iPhone only. iPad and Mac support may come in a future update.
