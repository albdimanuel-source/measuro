Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

🔒 Overview

Measuro is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store or sell any personal data. This policy explains what data is used and why.

Measuro does not require an account and does not transmit personal information to our servers.

📊 Advertising

Measuro displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

You can remove all ads permanently with the one-time in-app purchase.

💳 Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchase status is verified locally through StoreKit.

📱 Data stored on device

Measuro stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your unit order and favourites preferences
  • In-app purchase status (ad-free)

This data never leaves your device.

🌐 Third-party services

Measuro uses the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases
  • App Tracking Transparency — required by Apple for ad tracking consent

👶 Children

Measuro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

✉️ Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the Support page.
